Growing up, I was fascinated by how entrepreneurs like Jeff Bezos built platforms that could reach millions with a single click. From early on I knew I wanted to combine technology and business — which is why I chose B.Com in Computer Applications at Osmania University, Hyderabad, studying computers, accounting, business statistics, and business law together.

After graduating I joined Retunes IT Media as an intern and was later promoted to Software Consultant. I built web applications for the Government of Andhra Pradesh — handling client requirements, data management, application deployment, and client communication. That experience showed me how data and information directly shape organizational decisions.

I came to Northeastern University, Boston to build analytical and systems thinking skills. I completed my Informatics Capstone as the Analytical Design Lead for the Feminine Intelligence Agency — a live sponsor project studying whether AI can detect emotional manipulation. I am now actively building expertise in SAP IBP on a live server, focused on Demand Planning, S&OP, Supply Planning, and Control Tower, with a long-term goal of becoming a SAP IBP Consultant.
