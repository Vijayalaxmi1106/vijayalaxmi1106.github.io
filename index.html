Growing up, I was inspired by how entrepreneurs like Jeff Bezos built businesses that could reach millions with a single click. From early on I knew I wanted to combine technology and business — which is why I chose B.Com in Computer Applications at Osmania University.

After graduating I worked at Retunes IT Media as a Software Consultant, building web applications for the Government of Andhra Pradesh. That experience showed me how data and information directly shape organizational decisions.

I came to Northeastern University, Boston to build analytical and systems thinking skills. Now I am actively building expertise in SAP IBP — demand planning, S&OP, supply planning — with a goal of becoming a SAP IBP Consultant and eventually starting my own business.
